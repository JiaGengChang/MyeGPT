Table name: `stand_alone_survival`
Description: Right-censored survival information of each patient (overall survival; OS, progression free survival; PFS) as well as various treatment lines and treatment responses (disease progression). PFS by treatment line is also available.
Format: column_number | field_name | description
# Identifier columns
1|public_id|Index column. Patient public identifier. MMRF_ followed by 4 unique digits. e.g. MMRF_1234
# Data columns related to treatment response or failure
21|bestresp|Best overall response
22|brespdy|Date of best overall response
# Fields related to first response (FR) to the line.  A score from 1 (poorest response) to 6 (best response).
23|fresp|First response
24|lineresp|Line of FR
25|frespdy|Date of FR
26|fstresp1|Line 1 FR
27|fstresp2|Line 2 FR

29|fstresp4|Line 4 FR
30|fstresp5|Line 5 FR

35|frespdy4|Line 4 date of FR
36|frespdy5|Line 5 date of FR
37|frespdy9|Line 9 date of FR
