Table name: `per_patient`
Description: A table containing general patient-level information such as clinical and demographic background of the patients. Also contains patient-level information on treatment type, stem cell transplant, ISS stage, risk markers, and death-related info.
Format: column_number|column_name|description
# Identifier field
1|public_id|Index column. Patient public identifier. MMRF_ followed by 4 unique digits. e.g. MMRF_1234
# Fields related to cancer staging (ISS), risk, and functional status 
23|imwg_risk_class|Risk classification by the International Myeloma Working Group
24|prolif_index|Proliferation index, a scalar value derived from RNASeq
49|d_pt_iss|ISS (International staging system), or cancer stage. integer. Code: 1 = Stage I, 2 = Stage II, 3 = Stage III.
55|creatinine|Creatinine level. For detailed info refer to `per_visit` table.
56|ecog|Baseline ECOG performance status. For detailed info refer to `per_visit` table.
